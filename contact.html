Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you deploy a liquid cooling solution?

Deployment timelines vary depending on the scale and complexity of your data center. Typically, our solutions can be implemented in 4-12 weeks from initial assessment to full operation. Our team works closely with you to create a deployment schedule that minimizes disruption to your operations.

Do you offer installation services?

Yes, we provide comprehensive installation services for all our cooling solutions. Our experienced technicians handle everything from initial setup to testing and commissioning. We also offer training for your staff to ensure smooth operation after installation.

Are your cooling solutions compatible with our existing infrastructure?

Our solutions are designed with flexibility in mind and can be adapted to work with various existing data center setups. We begin with a thorough assessment of your current infrastructure to determine the most suitable approach. Our hybrid cooling options are particularly well-suited for integration with existing cooling systems.

What maintenance is required for liquid cooling systems?

Liquid cooling systems typically require less maintenance than traditional air cooling. Regular maintenance includes fluid quality checks, filter replacements, and system inspections. We offer comprehensive maintenance plans with scheduled service visits to ensure your system operates at peak efficiency.
