Our Cooling Solutions

Innovative liquid cooling technologies for modern data centers

Why Liquid Cooling?

As data centers continue to increase in density and computing power, traditional air cooling methods are reaching their limits. Liquid cooling offers a superior alternative with significant advantages:

  • Up to 50% reduction in energy consumption
  • Significantly lower PUE (Power Usage Effectiveness)
  • Higher density server deployments
  • Reduced noise levels
  • Extended hardware lifespan
  • Smaller physical footprint

Our Liquid Cooling Solutions

Direct Liquid Cooling (DLC)

Our DLC solutions bring coolant directly to the heat sources, using specialized cold plates attached to CPUs, GPUs, and other high-heat components. This approach maximizes efficiency by removing heat at its source.

  • Ideal for high-performance computing
  • Supports up to 100kW per rack
  • Compatible with most server architectures
Learn More

Immersion Cooling

Our immersion cooling systems submerge entire servers in a dielectric fluid that efficiently transfers heat away from all components simultaneously. This approach offers the highest cooling efficiency available today.

  • Maximum thermal efficiency
  • Zero evaporation or oxidation
  • Eliminates need for fans and heat sinks
Learn More

Hybrid Cooling Systems

Our hybrid solutions combine the best aspects of liquid and air cooling, offering a gradual transition path for existing data centers. This approach allows for incremental adoption of liquid cooling technologies.

  • Minimal disruption to operations
  • Optimized for retrofitting existing facilities
  • Scalable implementation options
Learn More
